Nursing Stats – Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Updated October 5, 2025 | Contact: nursingstats1@gmail.com

1. Overview

Nursing Stats is a professional, offline calculator built for nursing administrators and healthcare leaders. It helps you calculate Per Patient Day (PPD), total staffing hours, and compliance-ready summaries while keeping your data secure on your device.

We respect your privacy and are committed to complete transparency about how our app works.

2. Data Collection & Storage

Nursing Stats does not collect, store, share, or transmit any user data off your device.

All information entered—such as facility names, census numbers, and shift counts—is stored locally using Apple’s secure UserDefaults system. No cloud storage, analytics, or tracking occurs.

3. Third-Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob to display non-personalized ads. AdMob may access limited technical information (like device type and region) only to deliver safe, contextual advertising. No personal identifiers or behavioral profiles are collected or shared.

You can learn more by reviewing Google’s Privacy Policy.

4. Sharing & Printing

When you generate or print reports, all actions occur through Apple’s native iOS frameworks. The app does not upload or store printed content anywhere. You maintain full control over what you share or print.

5. User Responsibility & Compliance

Nursing Stats is a professional tool to assist with staffing calculations and PPD analysis. It is not a substitute for official regulatory reporting or CMS compliance documentation.

You are responsible for verifying staffing data and ensuring accuracy before posting or submitting reports.

6. Your Control & Data Management

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can clear it anytime by deleting and reinstalling the app. Nursing Stats has no access to or backup of your entries.

7. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect feature improvements or regulatory changes. All updates will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or how Nursing Stats protects your privacy, please email us at nursingstats1@gmail.com.
