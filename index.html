Last Updated: October 5, 2025

1. Overview

Nursing Stats is a secure, offline staffing calculator designed for healthcare professionals. The app helps you calculate Per Patient Day (PPD) and total staffing hours accurately and privately. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data Collection

Nursing Stats does not collect, store, or share any personal or usage data externally. All information you enter—such as facility names, census numbers, and staffing data—is stored locally on your device using Apple’s AppStorage (secure UserDefaults). None of your data ever leaves your device.
